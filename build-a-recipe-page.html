Slop

This recipe one of the recipes EVER! I love this stuff.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Slime
  • Goop
  • Cornstarch
  • Baking Soda
  • Mirapoix

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredents (in no particular order or amount) into a big cauldron.
  2. Add a dash of love
  3. Bake at 10,000C for whatever amount of time you feel
  4. Let cool until it hardens like cement
  5. Enjoy!
